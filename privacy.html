ANTICS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

TL;DR: Antics has no accounts, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. We never know who you are. The only data that exists is a random anonymous ID, used to unlock purchases and power referral codes.

What we don't collect

Antics has no user accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising and no tracking. We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, or anything that identifies you. What you play, what you answer, and who you play with is never recorded.

What stays on your device

To make the app work between sessions, Antics saves a small amount of information in your phone's local storage only:

  • Your confirmation that you are 18 or over
  • Your chosen game mode (Drinking or Sober)
  • Any player names you type in

This information never leaves your device and is permanently removed when you delete the app.

Purchases

Payments are handled entirely by Apple ‚Äî we never see your payment details. To unlock what you've bought (and restore it if you reinstall), the app uses RevenueCat, a purchase-management service. RevenueCat receives a randomly generated anonymous ID and your purchase history for this app. That ID is not linked to your name, Apple ID, email, or any other identity, and it is not used for advertising or shared with anyone else. See the RevenueCat privacy policy.

Referral codes

If you use the invite feature, our server generates a referral code linked to the same anonymous ID and stores it, along with which codes have been redeemed, so that rewards can be granted fairly. When a code is redeemed, the app also sends Apple a DeviceCheck token ‚Äî a mechanism built by Apple that lets us ask "has this iPhone redeemed a code before?" without telling us anything about the device or its owner. We never receive any device identifier, and none of this referral data can be traced back to you personally.

Third parties

The complete list of services involved in running Antics:

  • Apple ‚Äî payments and DeviceCheck
  • RevenueCat ‚Äî purchase unlocking and restore
  • Supabase ‚Äî hosts the referral-code database

None of them receive anything that identifies you. There is no advertising or analytics software in the app at all.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you can ask what data is held and ask for it to be deleted. Because everything is keyed to an anonymous ID, if you contact us we may need you to read that ID out of the app so we can find the records. Deleting the app removes everything stored on your device; emailing us lets you clear any referral records too.

Age requirement

Antics is designed for adults aged 18 and over and carries a corresponding App Store age rating. Some game modes reference alcohol; please drink responsibly and look after each other.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Antics changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the change will be clearly described here.
