Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

TL;DR: Antics collects no personal data. Nothing you do in the app ever leaves your phone.

What we collect

Nothing. Antics has no user accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party data collection of any kind. The app works entirely on your device and does not transmit your information anywhere.

What stays on your device

To make the app work between sessions, Antics saves a small amount of information in your phone's local storage only:

  • Your confirmation that you are 18 or over
  • Your chosen game mode (Drinking or Sober)
  • Any player names you type in

This information never leaves your device, is never sent to us or to anyone else, and is permanently removed when you delete the app.

Third parties

Antics contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software. Your use of the app is not shared with anyone because it is not recorded in the first place.

Age requirement

Antics is designed for adults aged 18 and over and carries a corresponding App Store age rating. Some game modes reference alcohol; please drink responsibly and look after each other.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Antics ever changes how data is handled (for example, if optional online features are added), this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the change will be clearly described here.
