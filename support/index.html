Contact

For any problem, question, refund query or idea, email support@anticsapp.com. Replies normally come within a day or two.

Restore your purchase

Premium is tied to your Apple Account, not your phone. If you've reinstalled the app or switched devices:

  • Make sure the device is signed into the Apple Account you bought with
  • Open the premium screen in Antics and tap Restore Purchases
  • Still locked? Email us and we'll sort it

Refunds are handled by Apple: go to reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple Account.

Referral codes

A few rules that catch people out:

  • Each iPhone can redeem one referral code, ever
  • You can't redeem your own code
  • Codes stop working once the sharer hits their monthly reward limit

If a code still won't go through, email us with the code and we'll look it up.
